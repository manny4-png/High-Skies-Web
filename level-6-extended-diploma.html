About the Program

Shaping Leaders in Management (Finance)

This program is equivalent to a Bachelor’s Degree and has been designed as an alternative route to professional development with the shortest possible time. Within 12 months, one can achieve a qualification equivalent to a bachelor’s degree and further purse the 1-year Master degree at High Skies College.

Why Choose This Program?

  • Local and International Recognition: ATHE-accredited, UK-recognised qualification
  • Students who complete this qualification can progress to Level 7 Extended Diploma (equivalent to a Post Graduate Diploma) or 1 year Master’s degree at High Skies College or any of our international partner universities such as Bolton, Coventry, Birmingham City, Leeds Trinity and Northwood (Michigan)
  • Flexible study options: Lectures are held one-weekend per month, the rest are online
  • Our admission process is flexible and career-focused, offering a professional pathway to internationally recognized certification. It provides an alternative to traditional university admission, with clear progression from Certificate to Diploma and up to MBA level. For assistance, please contact our Admissions Manager.
  • Affordable with flexible Payment Plan
Program Details

Athe level 6 Extended Diploma in Management (Finance) is a 120-credit qualification. This qualification is equivalent to a Bachelor Degree. It is regulated by OFQUAL-UK and awarded by the prestigious Awards for Training and Higher Education (ATHE) in the UK and in collaboration with High Skies College-Ghana. The Course content are:



MANDATORY UNITS

1. Leadership and Management

2. Research Project

3. Managing Quality and Service Delivery

4. Personal Leadership and Management Development

5. Marketing Communications

6. Financial Decision Making for Managers

7. Human Resource Management

8. Managing Change

HIGH SKIES/ATHE LEVEL 6 ENTRY REQUIREMENT

For entry into the Level 6 Program, applicants should normally meet at least one of the following criteria:

1. West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE): Passes in three Core Subjects and three elective subjects including Mathematics and English Language

2. Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (SSSCE): Passes in three Core Subjects and three elective subjects including Mathematics and English Language

3. Ordinary Level or Advanced Level Certificates

4. Diploma or Higher National Diploma

5. Any qualification equivalent to any of the above

The following documents should be provided in addition to the application form:

1. Copy of Original certificate

2. Two passport photographs



(Please note that all certificates and results slips other than the WASSCE must be referred to the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission for verification and evaluation to determine their eligibility for admission into High Skies. Call our admissions manager for support and advice)
