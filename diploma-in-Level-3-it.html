About the Program

Your Gateway to the Digital World

We open the world to you with a simple and well – tailored Professional Program in Information and Digital Technologies that is recognized all over the world.

The level 3 Diploma in Information and Digital Technologies is equivalent to 1st year of any local and international University.

It is regulated by OFQUAL-UK and awarded by the prestigious Awards for Training and Higher Education (ATHE) in the UK and Collaboration with High Skies College-Ghana.

Why Choose This Program?

  • Local and International Recognition: ATHE-accredited, UK-recognised qualification
  • Students who complete this qualification can progress to Level 5 Extended Diploma or bachelor’s degree at one of our local Universities or International partner universities such as Bolton, Coventry, Birmingham City, Leeds Trinity and Northwood (Michigan)
  • Flexible study options: Lectures are held one-weekend per month, the rest are online
  • Our admission process is flexible and career-focused, offering a professional pathway to internationally recognized certification. It provides an alternative to traditional university admission, with clear progression from Certificate to Diploma and up to MBA level. For assistance, please contact our Admissions Manager.
  • Affordable with flexible Payment Plan
Program Details

Mandatory Units

To qualify for the 120-credit Diploma in Information and Digital Technologies, one must complete ALL mandatory units in twelve (12) months.

1. Introduction to Computer Programming

2. Introduction to Computing Mathematics

3. Introduction to Cyber Security

4. Introduction to Digital Technologies

5. Introduction to Emerging Technologies

6. Introduction to Mobile Application Development

7. Introduction to Web Development

HIGH SKIES/ATHE LEVEL 3 ENTRY REQUIREMENT

1. WASSCE (Senior High School Certificate) or equivalent qualification. Six subjects are used to calculate your standard WASSCE score: Integrated Science, Core Math, Core English and three electives. A minimum grade of C6 in each subject is required to apply.

2. MATURE APPLICANTS (Applicants aged 25 or above)

3. IGCSE/GCE ‘O’ Level and at least three ‘A’ Level passes (with grade A-D)

4. International Baccalaureate(IB) Diploma

5. French Baccalaureate (a certified copy of the original score and a certified translated copy in English)

6. Other equivalent exam results approved by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission.

7. National Vocational Training Institute (NVTI) part Three

(Please note that all certificates and results slips other than the WASSCE must be referred to the National Accreditation Board for verification, evaluation and to determine their eligibility for admission into High Skies)
