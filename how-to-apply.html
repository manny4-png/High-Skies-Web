About our college and why you should choose us.

We believe in better and relevant education for Africa

High Skies Education is aimed at birthing a new breed of African Leaders capable of creating jobs, questioning the status quo and managing change in their organizations. This uniqueness of High Skies education PLACES emphasis on entrepreneurship with the aim of creating new ventures, leadership development, critical thinking skills, ethical standards in doing business, continued community engagement.

Through our programs, students are exposed to courses that focuses on the uniqueness of African business strategy, management, finance, e-commerce, leadership, diversity and people skills with the aim of helping the student understand terrain in which they find themselves and master the art of turning these to their advantage. We are set to help students appreciate Africa’s past and the forces that has shaped the continent for decades and how to change the course of the future in terms of business and leadership on our continent.

High Skies is a private tertiary institution located in the harbor city of Tema. It was registered in April 2012 as a company limited by guarantee and duly authorized by the National Accreditation Board to initiate and operate as a tertiary institution.
