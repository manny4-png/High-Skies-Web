Message from the Founder

A vision for Africa's educational transformation and economic independence

Philip Ankamafio Mensah

Founder & Visionary

High Skies College

Africa has always been the focus of global brand and business interests. However, we haven't competed well in the past. This is because our education was designed to maintain the status quo of our colonial masters.

Even though we are independent politically, economic and spiritual independence has been an illusion. But now is the time.

With the right education, mindset and a stronger sense of purpose, Africa can be truly free. We believe in better and relevant education for Africa.

This is what we need now!

Our Vision for Africa

High Skies College was born out of a deep conviction that education is the key to unlocking Africa's potential. We are committed to developing a new generation of leaders who will drive positive change across the continent.

Through our programs, we aim to equip students with not just academic knowledge, but also the practical skills, ethical foundation, and innovative thinking needed to transform Africa's business landscape and create sustainable prosperity for all.

Philip Ankamafio Mensah

Founder

Education for Transformation
