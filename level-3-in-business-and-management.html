We open up the world to you with simple and well-tailored professional Diploma Program in Business and Management that is recognized in United Kingdom and other parts of the world. This program is the equivalent level to the first year of a Bachelor’s degree in Management. It has been designed for students who wish to pursue junior and middle level management offices and to purse full bachelor’s degree even though they may not have the traditional entry qualifications usually required.

Why Choose This Course  

Local and International Recognition: This program is recognized by the National Accreditation of Ghana and The Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation of the United Kingdom (UK) through the Awards For Training and Higher Education (ATHE) in the UK.

Easy Academic Progression: Students who complete this qualification can progress to Level 5 Extended Diploma or study a variety of bachelor’s degrees at one of our local Universities or International partner universities such as Bolton, Coventry, Birmingham City, Leeds Trinity and Northwood (Michigan) in subjects such as Financial Management, Business Studies, Law, and Technology for Business, Organizational Leadership and beyond.

Admission Criteria: Our admission plan is simple and flexible. It follows the professional route in achieving internationally recognized certification and provides a workable alternative to the traditional system of University admission. This route provides a build up from Certificate, Diploma and feeds into the MBA level. (Please call our admissions manager for support)

Simple Learning alternatives: Students and Learners would have lectures once a month on the weekends. The rest are done online where necessary. 

Affordable with flexible Payment Plan: Well fee plan that allows systematic payments

To qualify for the 120-credit extended diploma, one must complete ALL 4 mandatory units and ANY 4 of the optional units 

Mandatory Units

Managing Business Operations

Maximising Resources to Achieve Business Success

The Business Environment

Managing People in Organisations

Working in Teams

Effective Business Communication 

Progression Routes

On successful completion of a Level 3 Diploma in Business and Management there are a number of progression opportunities. Learners may progress to:

1. Degree programme in any local and International university 

2. Employment or setting up one’s own business

3. ATHE Level 4 Extended Diploma in Business and Management

4. It serves as a foundational program for Mature students pursuing higher education

HIGH SKIES/ATHE LEVEL ENTRY REQUIREMENT

Applicant must have ATHE level 3 Diploma in Business and Management

West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE); Six subjects are used to calculate your standard WASSCE score: Integrated Science, Core Math, Core English and three electives. A minimum grade of C6 in each subject is required to apply. 

IGCSE/GCE ‘O’ Level and at least three ‘A’ Level passes (with grade A-D)

International Baccalaureate(IB) Diploma

French Baccalaureate (a certified copy of the original score and a certified translated copy in English)

Other equivalent exam results approved by the National Accreditation Board of Ghana

National Vocational Training Institute (NVTI) part Three.

(Please note that all certificates and results slips other than the WASSCE must be referred to the National Accreditation Board for verification, evaluation and to determine their eligibility for admission into High Skies)
