WHO WE ARE

Empowering Leaders, Transforming Africa

High Skies College, also known as High Skies, is a private tertiary institution located in the Port City of Tema. It was registered in April 2012 as a company limited by guarantee and duly authorized by the National Accreditation Board to initiate and operate as a tertiary institution.

The birth of High Skies was informed by the need to support the development of Ghana's human resource base in the wake of rapid economic and political awareness and to provide qualified manpower to meet the needs of the growing Ghanaian economy and that of Africa as a whole.

Our prestigious MBA and Executive Education programs are designed for the working class and anyone who wants to improve their working skills, network with high caliber industry practitioners, and prepare themselves for higher and challenging roles in their organizations.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading college in business leadership, science, and technology—built on ethics, innovation, and global relevance.

Our Mission

The mission of High Skies College is to develop human potential by fostering innovation, ethical leadership, and practical solutions that connect Africa's business environment to the global village.
