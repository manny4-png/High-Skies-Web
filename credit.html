Local Accreditations & Affiliations

Recognised and authorised by Ghana's leading educational and professional bodies

Local

Ghana Tertiary Education Commission

GTEC — Ghana

High Skies College is duly registered and authorised by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission to initiate and operate as a tertiary institution. GTEC is the statutory body responsible for accrediting tertiary institutions and programmes in Ghana.

  • Full institutional accreditation
  • All programmes approved
  • Registered since 2012
University Partner

University of Cape Coast

UCC — Ghana

Our prestigious MBA program is a collaboration between High Skies College and the University of Cape Coast. HSC provides the tuition while UCC awards the degrees, giving students a qualification from one of Ghana's most respected universities.

  • MBA degree awarded by UCC
  • Joint programme structure
  • Fully recognised degree
Local

National Board for Professional & Technical Examinations

NABPTEX — Ghana

NABPTEX is responsible for the conduct and certification of professional and technical examinations in Ghana. Our programmes align with NABPTEX standards, ensuring students receive professionally recognised qualifications.

  • Professional examinations body
  • Technical certification
  • Industry-aligned standards

International Accreditations

Our qualifications are recognised by leading UK regulatory and awarding bodies — opening doors to universities worldwide

UK Regulatory

Office of Qualifications & Examinations Regulation

Ofqual — United Kingdom

Ofqual is the official government regulator of qualifications, examinations and assessments in England. Our affiliation through ATHE means our programmes meet the rigorous standards set by this UK government body, giving our qualifications global credibility.

  • UK government regulator
  • Standards-compliant programmes
  • Global recognition
UK Awarding Body

Awards for Training & Higher Education

ATHE — United Kingdom

ATHE is a UK-based awarding organisation regulated by Ofqual. ATHE qualifications are approved as progression pathways to relevant Bachelor's and Master's Top-Up degrees by a growing number of UK and international universities — providing significant tertiary education opportunities for our students.

  • Regulated by Ofqual, UK
  • Pathway to UK university degrees
  • Recognised by international universities
